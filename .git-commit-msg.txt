IRON VOLLEY: playable build — 5 maps, 4 tanks, AI war, specials, split-screen

3D tank artillery combat (Three.js, zero build step). Working game loop
verified headless on every map.

- Terrain: analytic heightfield (mesh + physics share one function),
  vertex-color painting, slope rock blending, water/lava/energy planes,
  gradient sky dome shader w/ sun + stars, per-map prop kits.
- Maps: Dune Sea, Frostline (frozen lake + snowfall), Verdant Vale
  (river valley), Cinder Peak (volcano + lava channels + embers),
  Neon Rift (terraced synthwave canyon, wireframe glow, monoliths).
- Tanks: JACKAL/VIPER/BASTION/LONGBOW — procedural chassis (hull,
  turret, barrel, mounted MG, tracks, headlights), arcade physics over
  the heightfield w/ slope resistance + collisions.
- Weapons: ballistic shells (shared launch-angle solver), hold-to-fire
  MG w/ heat, special rounds — SCATTER (9 bomblets), LANCE laser,
  NUKE, INFERNO fire pools (DoT), SINGULARITY gravity well (pulls
  tanks AND bends shells).
- Pickups: beacon crates, 5 live at once, respawning.
- AI: target selection, charge/standoff/orbit movement, terrain +
  obstacle feelers, stop-to-fire artillery doctrine with high-arc
  lobbing over hills, MG at knife range, crate seeking, 3 difficulties.
- Shell: menu flow (mode/tank/bots/difficulty/map), split-screen
  scissor rendering + dual keyboard maps, per-player chase cam w/
  trauma shake + death orbit cam, HUD (hp/reload/heat/special/toasts),
  kill feed, score pill, end screen standings, re-deploy.
- Audio (Codex CLI): full procedural WebAudio kit — cannon/MG/laser/
  nuke/fires/UI/engine loops + menu & battle music schedulers.
- Effects (Codex CLI): pooled particles — muzzle/tracers/explosions/
  shock rings/nuke mushroom/fire pools/wreck smoke/dust + snow & ember
  ambience.
- Tests: Playwright screenshot harness (7 shots, zero console errors)
  + headless all-bot playtest (21 checks: combat on all maps, kills,
  respawns, pickups, specials, match end) — ALL PASSING.
